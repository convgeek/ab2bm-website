## Audience & Infrastructure

### Global Reach, Local Coverage
AdvanceB2BMedia's audience spans 48.5 million B2B professionals across four major regions:

- **North America:** 22M
- **EMEA:** 14.7M
- **APAC:** 9.9M
- **LATAM:** 1.9M
- **Total Audience:** 48.5M

### Audience-First Approach
Rather than relying on a static database of contacts, AdvanceB2BMedia has built an engaged audience model. The difference matters: engaged audiences convert at meaningfully higher rates than cold database records.

The audience-building methodology centers on six interconnected activities:

- **Account Coverage** — ensuring the right companies are represented across target markets
- **Key Contacts, Roles & Titles** — mapping decision-makers and buying committee members
- **Opt-In Outreach** — putting the B2B professional in control of the engagement
- **Info Validation & Verification** — confirming professional information is current and accurate
- **Data/Record Enhancement** — adding data and intelligence as professionals engage
- **Audience Segmentation** — enabling precise targeting for every program

### What Makes the Audience Different
- A decade spent building an opt-in database and trust with B2B professionals
- B2B professionals and prospects remain in control of the relationship
- Content is identified and fed based on digital behavior and activity
- Professionals opt in, validate, and regularly update their own information
- Data and intelligence are continuously added as professionals engage

## Core Demand Programs

### 1. ADVANCE ENGAGE

**Tagline:** Top-of-funnel opt-in lead generation built around your content.

**Solution Overview**
ADVANCE ENGAGE is a popular program built for marketers who need to generate opt-in leads that engage and who meet specific business and buyer criteria. It is the go-to program for content syndication and top-of-funnel demand.

**How the Program Works**
- Puts high-value, educational content to work engaging B2B professionals who meet the target profile
- Uses multiple communications channels to engage buyers on their terms
- The AB2B team advises on content strategy and handles all outreach and program management

**What You Get**
- Top-of-funnel, opt-in contacts who have engaged with client content and fit specific buyer criteria
- A dedicated landing page aligned with client brand standards
- Turnkey campaign and consultative program management, including reporting and ROI optimization
- Optional add-ons: custom or profiling questions, 2-touch engagement, email nurture, and/or SIC and NAICS codes plus intent data

**Best For**
Demand generation and content marketing teams that need to fill the top of the funnel with engaged, opt-in prospects and have existing educational content assets ready to promote. ADVANCE ENGAGE is a strong fit for:

- **Cybersecurity, identity, and data protection vendors** reaching IT and security professionals with educational content before introducing a specific solution
- **Cloud, DevOps, and IT operations vendors** targeting technical buyers who research extensively before engaging sales
- **Data, analytics, and AI/ML vendors** building category awareness and educating the market on emerging approaches
- **MarTech, SalesTech, and RevOps vendors** reaching marketing and revenue leaders across the enterprise
- **FinTech and HR Tech vendors** engaging finance, HR, and people leaders with category-specific content
- **Enterprise SaaS and platform vendors** building awareness for a new product, category, or positioning shift
- **Marketing teams with strong content libraries** (eBooks, whitepapers, webinars, guides) that need distribution beyond owned channels
- **Vendors in competitive or crowded categories** where building early awareness is the prerequisite to getting shortlisted later

### 2. ADVANCE ABM

**Tagline:** Account-based engagement that fuels your ABM strategy.

**Solution Overview**
ADVANCE ABM fuels and feeds account-based marketing strategies by generating opt-in, engaged buying committee leads within a client's target account list.

**How the Program Works**
- Starts by assessing and activating the client's target or named account list, regardless of size
- Adds to and generates the target account list using look-alike modeling and intent data
- Uses precise targeting to identify and engage key buying committee roles and titles
- The AB2B team advises on ABM campaign strategy, targeting, and optimization

**What You Get**
- 1- or 2-touch verified buying committee leads based on the target account list
- Account and buyer intelligence generated during campaign outreach and engagement
- A dedicated program manager from kickoff through campaign delivery
- Campaign reporting that includes account and buyer engagement data

**Best For**
Marketing teams running account-based strategies who need to identify and engage buying committee members at target accounts with precision. ADVANCE ABM is a strong fit for:

- **Enterprise and mid-market B2B tech vendors** with defined target account lists and multi-stakeholder buying processes
- **Cybersecurity, identity, and infrastructure vendors** whose deals involve stakeholders across IT, security, compliance, and the executive suite
- **High-ACV enterprise SaaS and platform vendors** where land-and-expand depends on engaging the full buying committee, not just one champion
- **Data, AI, and analytics vendors** selling into enterprise buying committees that span engineering, data, and business leadership
- **FinTech, MarTech, and HR Tech vendors** targeting specific departmental buyers inside enterprise accounts
- **Marketing teams partnered with sales on named accounts** who need to feed SDRs and AEs with engaged contacts at the right companies
- **Vendors expanding into new segments or verticals** who need to build target account lists using look-alike modeling and intent data

### 3. ADVANCE INSTALL

**Tagline:** Leads at organizations with the specific installed technology you need.

**Solution Overview**
ADVANCE INSTALL identifies organizations with specific installed technologies and environment attributes, then generates engaged, opt-in leads that match the client's buyer profile at those organizations.

**How the Program Works**
- Uses technographic data to identify organizations with specific technology installed or in use
- Conducts targeted outreach to companies with the identified installed tech that also fit the target profile
- The AB2B team handles all aspects of program delivery to meet client KPIs

**What You Get**
- Opt-in leads at companies with specific technology in use that also match target profile criteria
- A dedicated landing page with installed-base qualification questions
- Full-service program management, including campaign reporting and lead delivery to the client's system of choice
- Optional add-ons: custom and profiling account questions, 2-touch outreach, and/or email nurture

**Best For**
Brands that market and sell into organizations requiring specific installed technologies — where the presence (or absence) of a particular platform fundamentally defines whether a prospect is a fit. ADVANCE INSTALL is a strong fit for:

- **Vendors whose solutions extend or integrate with a specific platform** (e.g., Microsoft 365, Salesforce, ServiceNow, AWS, Azure, Google Cloud, VMware, SAP, Workday, Microsoft Entra)
- **Backup, data protection, and disaster recovery vendors** who need to reach organizations using specific cloud platforms or production systems
- **Security tooling vendors** (EDR, XDR, SIEM, IAM, CNAPP, DSPM) whose ICP depends on what the prospect has already deployed
- **Cloud, DevOps, and observability vendors** whose value depends on a specific cloud provider, container platform, or CI/CD stack being in place
- **Data and AI tooling vendors** whose ICP depends on the customer running a particular data warehouse, lakehouse, or ML platform
- **MarTech, SalesTech, and RevOps vendors** targeting users of a specific CRM, marketing automation, or CDP platform
- **Rip-and-replace competitors** targeting users of a named incumbent solution
- **Add-on, plug-in, and middleware vendors** whose value proposition depends on the underlying platform being deployed

### 4. ADVANCE BANT

**Tagline:** Sales-ready leads qualified on Budget, Authority, Need, and Timing.

**Solution Overview**
ADVANCE BANT delivers leads within organizations that meet the target profile and have confirmed they have Budget, Authority, Need, and Timing — and are actively looking for solutions in the client's market category within the next six months.

**How the Program Works**
- Collaborative co-creation of BANT criteria and qualification questions for optimal targeting and outreach
- Prospective buyers are BANT qualified using digital and tele-touch within the opt-in universe
- The AB2B team handles all aspects of campaign setup, outreach, and program delivery to hit success metrics

**What You Get**
- Verified, qualified leads that match the ideal ICP criteria, generated using the client's content assets and profiled for budget, role in the purchase process, and timing
- All leads have answered specific, custom questions covering Budget, Authority, Need, and Timeframe
- An "open" field that captures additional intelligence delivered with each lead for detailed BDR and sales follow-up
- Full-service program management, including campaign reporting and lead delivery to the client's system
- Coaching and communications scripting for optimal outreach and follow-up

**Best For**
Sales-led organizations that need pipeline-ready leads and want to shorten the sales cycle by starting conversations with prospects who have already confirmed intent to buy. ADVANCE BANT is a strong fit for:

- **Enterprise software, SaaS, and platform vendors with high ACVs** who can't afford to burn AE time on unqualified meetings
- **Cybersecurity, data protection, and IT infrastructure vendors** where sales cycles are long and pipeline conversion depends on early qualification
- **FinTech, MarTech, and HR Tech vendors** with defined ICPs and active, competitive pipelines
- **High-velocity inside sales teams** that need a steady flow of qualified opportunities to hit quota
- **BDR and SDR organizations** whose productivity depends on starting with warm, pre-qualified conversations rather than cold prospecting
- **Marketing teams under pressure to show pipeline contribution** who need program-level KPIs tied directly to sales-accepted leads
- **Vendors launching in new regions or segments** who need qualified pipeline fast without building a BDR team from scratch

### 5. ADVANCE EXPAND

**Tagline:** Cross-sell, upsell, and expansion inside your current customer base.

**Solution Overview**
ADVANCE EXPAND is for marketing teams charged with expanding, cross-selling, and upselling solutions into their current customer bases — reaching new decision-makers, new geographies, and new business units within existing accounts.

**How the Program Works**
- Reviews, builds, and applies appropriate inclusion and suppression lists for precision customer targeting
- Puts the client's high-value, educational content to work engaging B2B professionals who meet the target profile within customer accounts
- The AB2B team advises on content strategy and handles all outreach and program management

**What You Get**
- Opt-in, verified leads who work within current customer organizations and fit specific profile criteria
- A dedicated landing page with targeted outreach aligned with client brand standards
- Full-service program management, including campaign reporting and lead delivery to client systems
- Optional add-ons: 2-touch engagement, custom and profiling questions, and/or installed base targeting

**Best For**
Customer marketing, account management, and expansion-focused teams tasked with growing revenue inside the existing customer base. ADVANCE EXPAND is a strong fit for:

- **SaaS and subscription vendors** whose growth model depends on net revenue retention, cross-sell, and upsell
- **Platform vendors with multiple product lines** (security suites, cloud platforms, data platforms, SalesTech/MarTech suites) looking to expand adoption beyond the initial department or use case
- **Cybersecurity and IT vendors** landing in one team (e.g., SecOps) who need to expand into adjacent teams (IT, compliance, GRC, identity)
- **Enterprise vendors landing in one business unit** who need to reach decision-makers in adjacent BUs, regions, or subsidiaries
- **Customer marketing and customer success teams** supporting account management with qualified pipeline into existing logos
- **Vendors launching new products, modules, or price tiers** who want to prioritize their existing customer base before going to net-new
- **Global vendors expanding into new geographies** inside multinational customer accounts

## Data Privacy & Compliance

**Positioning line:** Lawyers and audiences love our diligence, so will yours.

AdvanceB2BMedia's compliance posture is a core part of the value proposition. Every buyer and every lead is managed under a rigorous privacy and compliance framework.

### The Three Pillars of Compliance
- **Global Compliance** — full alignment with GDPR, CASL, and CCPA requirements, ensuring leads can be used safely across regions
- **Opting In / Permissioning** — every audience member has explicitly opted in and granted permission for marketing outreach
- **Opt-In Recording** — opt-in records are captured and retained for verification and audit purposes

This framework means leads delivered to clients are not just targeted — they are permissioned, verifiable, and defensible.
