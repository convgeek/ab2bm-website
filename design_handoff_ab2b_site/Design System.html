02 / Color

Royal purple. Signal orange.

The logo's saturated royal purple is the brand's primary surface and authority color. Orange — the italic "2" — is reserved for moments of signal: conversion, emphasis, data callouts. Warm, low-chroma neutrals sit under both so the brand colors keep their charge.

One screen, one conversion.

Restrained. Intentional.
